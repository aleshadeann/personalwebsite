W E L C O M E

My name is Alesha Dean-Weathersby, and I am from West Bloomfield, Michigan. I am completing my second year at the University of Southern California, and I plan to graduate with a major in Communications and minors in Web Design and Sociology. While at USC, I have gotten involved around campus by becoming a leader in my sorority, Alpha Phi, joining a student-led dance team, Xpressions Dance Company, and becoming a member for a black social organization, D Society.

My interests include dance, social justice activism both of which you can read more about on the passions page, in addition to hanging out with my friends, social media, and design technology. My favorite platforms are YouTube and Tiktok, and I love watching content about education, race, sexuality, mental health, socialism, dance, fashion, and beauty. I also LOVE listening to music and going to concerts and festivals. My favorite artists currently are Lana Del Rey, Son Lux, The Neighbourhood, and J.Cole.

I am really enjoying all of the opportunities that I have because of USC and living in LA, and I am excited to keep exploring all of my options. As of right now, however, I plan on moving to another major coity after graduation and finding a job in media or entertainment where I can combine my passions to make the world a more equitable place through technology. I can't wait to see where the next two years and more take me.
